Welcome To FoodFitness

FoodFitness is a web app that primarily focuses on physcical health in the aspect of food consumption.

    It has multiple functions for the user to use:
  • A nutrition label searcher: where the user can search for a food item and they will be given a nutrition label with the various information.
  • A calories tracker - where the user can record and track their caloric intake
  • A recipe searcher - the user can search recipes by inputting an ingredient or dish. The will be also presented with other information
